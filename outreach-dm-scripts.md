# Subb2 Pilot Outreach: DM Scripts

Goal of every first message: get a reply, not a signup. One personal line, one hook, one question. No link until they reply. Keep it under 60 words.

The offer, in one line: 10 handpicked creators get Subb2 free for 6 months and help shape the product.

---

## 1. Fitness / online coaches (Instagram DM)

### Opener A: the free group angle
> Hey [name], been following your [specific thing: challenge, check-in posts, client wins]. Quick question: that group you run for clients, is it free right now?

*(Two-step opener. Highest reply rate. Pitch only after they answer.)*

### Opener B: single message, direct
> Hey [name], love how you run your [challenge/group]. I'm building Subb2: it turns a WhatsApp group into a paid membership. One link, it handles checkout and adds or removes members automatically. You keep 100%. I'm picking 10 coaches to get it free for 6 months and shape it with me. Want the details?

### Opener C: story reply (highest response rate on IG)
> [Reply to a story about their group or client results]
> This is exactly why I'm building what I'm building. Do you charge for that group or is it free?

---

## 2. Trading / finance creators (X or Telegram DM)

### Opener A
> Saw your [recent thread/call]. I'm building Subb2: paid access to your Telegram or Discord with one link. Auto add on payment, auto remove on cancel. Flat fee, so you keep 100% instead of giving Whop a cut. Taking 10 creators free for 6 months before launch. Interested?

### Opener B: the fee angle
> Quick math: at $5k/mo, Whop takes $150 every month. Subb2 is flat, you keep all of it, and your members stay in your Telegram. I'm giving 10 early creators 6 months free. Want in?

---

## 3. Real estate / newcomer community leaders (LinkedIn or WhatsApp)

### Opener A
> Hi [name], I came across [their group/community] and it's exactly the kind of community I built Subb2 for. It lets you charge a monthly fee for a WhatsApp group: one link handles payment, and the bot manages who's in. I'm inviting 10 community builders to use it free for 6 months. Open to a quick look?

---

## Follow-up bump (3 days after opener, if no reply)

> No worries if this isn't for you. If the group is staying free forever, ignore me. If you've ever thought about charging for it, I'm holding a pilot spot for you till Friday.

*(One bump only. Never send a third message.)*

---

## When they reply: qualify, then send the link

Ask these two, in your own words:

1. Where does your community live today, and roughly how many people are in it?
2. If you charged tomorrow, what would you charge?

If they have an active group and a real audience, send:

> Here's the link: subb2.com. Grab your handle and I'll set you up personally this week.

---

## Objection one-liners

**"Why is it free? What's the catch?"**
> No catch, no card. I want 10 creators to break it, tell me what's missing, and be the first case studies. After 6 months it's $49/mo flat, cancel anytime.

**"My audience won't pay."**
> They already pay for less on Whop and Patreon. Start at $19/mo for your most engaged 20 people and see. Worst case you learn what they value.

**"How is this different from Patreon or Whop?"**
> Those move your members to their platform and take a cut. Subb2 leaves your members exactly where they are, in your group, and takes 0% of your revenue.

**"Does this break WhatsApp rules?"**
> It runs on the official WhatsApp Business API. No hacks, no burner numbers.

---

## Who qualifies for the 10 spots

- Runs an active free group or broadcast channel today (WhatsApp, Discord, Telegram, or Slack)
- 10K+ followers with real engagement, or a smaller list that already buys from them
- Posts consistently, replies to their community
- Bonus: already sells something (coaching, course, picks)
