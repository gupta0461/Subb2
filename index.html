FAQ

Your questions, answered

We've built integrations with WhatsApp, Discord, Slack, and Telegram to sync your members and collect recurring payments automatically. So all you do is connect your community to Subb2, set your price, and share your link (subb2.com/yourname). When someone taps it, they land on your page and set up a recurring payment. Payment received, we add them. Payment missed, we remove them. You do nothing else. No spreadsheets, no "please pay" DMs.

We're building Subb2 right now, and we're hand-picking our first 10 creators to build it with us. All we ask is your honest feedback: what works, what doesn't, what's missing. In return, Subb2 is free for your first 6 months, along with hands-on support whenever you need it.

As a pilot creator, you're sharing you valuable time, trust, and the honest feedback that makes this platform what it is. We never want to lose sight of that. So, when the pilot phase ends, we’ll sit down and set your price together. We only want to charge what you feel our service is genuinely worth to you.

Through your own Stripe account. You connect it to Subb2 once, and every subscriber payment goes straight into your account, not ours. Subb2 handles the rest: subscriptions, access, and adding and removing members.

They tap your subb2.com/username link, land on your page, and pay with Apple Pay, Google Pay, or a card. The moment the payment goes through, they're added to your community on WhatsApp, Discord, Slack, or Telegram.

Apple Pay, Google Pay, and all major credit and debit cards: Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

Every subscriber has their own Subb2 account. There they can see all the creators they've subscribed to and cancel any plan themselves, anytime.

The moment a payment fails or a subscription is cancelled, that member loses access to your community, so your content stays exclusive to the people who actually pay. If they pay again, they're added right back.

Your community belongs to you, not the platform you host it on. You can instantly export your complete list of members—along with their contact info and active plans so that you don't have to start over again from scratch

Yes, anytime. Subb2 is month to month with no lock-in. Export your subscriber list, take everything you've built.
